This mode lets you take turns writing your feelings on the same device:
  • Role A writes down their thoughts and feelings first
  • After finishing, pass the device to Role B
  • Role B writes their perspective (cannot see Role A's content)
  • Both statements are submitted together for deep AI analysis
💡 Tip: Focus on your own feelings while writing—no need to respond to the other person.
